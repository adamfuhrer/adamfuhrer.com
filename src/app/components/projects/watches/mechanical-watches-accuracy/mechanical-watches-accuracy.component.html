Accuracy of Mechanical Watches

Since a young age I’ve always had a fascination with watches — specifically mechanical watches. Mechanical watches are powered by a carefully regulated release of energy from a wound spring and use purely mechanical components to keep time. Being fortunate enough to own a few luxury watches, I wanted to test the accuracy of their timekeeping against their manufacturer’s listed precision. To measure this, I wore the watch every day for a month and set it to UTC time at the same day and time each week. The difference between time gained or lost during the week is recorded.
